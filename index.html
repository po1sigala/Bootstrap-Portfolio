Hi! I'm Alex

What have I Spent my time learning?

I received my B.S. in Biology with a concentration in molecular biology. Much of my work required the use of population modelling software. I also took a week long python introduction to familiarize myself with some statistical tools useful in biology

How did I get into coding?

I have always been interested in coding and creating on the web. My first experience came with HTML and CSS during the days of MySpace where I (albeit a little blindly) toyed with each to design my profile. In college I used NetLogo, a programmable modeling environment built with scala and Java, to emulate the growth of worms in vivo. After graduation I worked in a few different fields but found myself always coming back to coding. I took some courses on Udemy, CodeAcademy, etc. and always found them engaging and fun. Finally I decided that my hobby really should be my career considering the time I was putting into it and began reaching out to peers and collagues about the pros and cons they saw with different languages. I settled on JavaScript as my specialty for its extreme flexibility and its unique ability to be used on both front and backend apps, and now even on mobile with React Native.

What makes me stand out?

In addition to my skills with JavaScript and molecular bio techniques, I believe I have unique skill set in my ability to interact with and manage diverse groups of people compared to most engineers. I have worked on a manufacturing floor, as part of a new product launch team, on a remote field site testing products, in the lab testing specific hypothesis, and as a project manager creating a new web application. This wide range of experiences helps me to be a better team member because I understand how each part of a group has different pressures and goals. I am a firm believer that a strong team of solid individuals will outperform a weak team with some rockstars.

What am I like as a person?

In short, I am a goal oriented, personable, and passionate individual who wants to use his skills to best serve his community and the world as a whole. I have always wanted to have my work have far reaching impacts which has influenced my choice in jobs and education.
